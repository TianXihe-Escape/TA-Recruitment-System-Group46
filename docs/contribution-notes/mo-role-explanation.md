# Module Organiser Role Explanation

## Purpose

This note explains the responsibilities of the Module Organiser role in the system.

## Main responsibilities

The Module Organiser role manages recruitment activity for assigned modules and related support tasks.

## Typical MO actions

Module Organisers can:

- create and edit job postings
- review applicants
- inspect profiles and CV references
- compare required skills with applicant information
- shortlist applicants
- invite interviews
- accept or reject applications
- leave reviewer notes

## Why this role matters

The MO role connects the teaching side of the school to the recruitment workflow by defining support needs and making hiring decisions.

## Relationship to other roles

MOs review TA applications and their decisions later contribute to workload visibility and system oversight at the Admin level.
